Referral Policy

(Please note that during the description of rules the “Referrer” is termed the user who refers another user and “Referee” is the user who has been referred by a “Referrer”)

Fello runs a referral programme as one of its marketing initiatives. As a part of this referral program, users who have registered and signed up on the application can share their referral link which is accessible through the referral section of the application and invite users to signup on the platform. The referral link can be shared on any of the social media platforms and sources especially and not restricted to Whatsapp, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Reddit, any email application, text, etc

Referrer will be awarded Rs.25 and 10 tambola tickets as a part of the referral programme only if the following conditions are met.

  1. The Referee must signup on the platform through the link shared by the Referrer
  2. The Referee must save a minimum of Rs.100 on the platform
  3. The Referee should use the application after saving on the platform for a minimum period of one week

Along with the above conditions, the Referrer can claim and withdraw the referral award only if the below conditions are met:

  1. Upto Rs.200 can be claimed and withdrawn without saving on the platform through depositing based on the condition that the application is being used every day at least once and it is not uninstalled
  2. Above Rs.200 can be claimed only after saving a minimum of Rs.100 on the platform through depositing and using the application for a minimum of two weeks without being uninstalled.

The Referee will also be awarded Rs.25 and 10 tambola tickets. The Rs.25 can be claimed and withdrawn only after saving a minimum of Rs.100 on the platform through deposit.

Note:

The referral policy can be changed/terminated as per the interests of Fello.
